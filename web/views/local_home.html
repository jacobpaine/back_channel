LoCAL is a restaurant that is centered on growing the highest quality produce with only the best care. We support our local farmers and know each by name and every person who has grown anything that we serve for our meals. The local economy is the heart of our community and we will continue to engage in programs that promote more agricultural education.

LoCAL is aware of our responsiblity to the community to provide the healthiest options for our patrons. All of our meals are prepared with fresh produce, grown locally, and are not only the tastiest option, but the healthiest as well.
